Who we are?

Paradise Travels (Pvt) Ltd. is one of Sri Lanka's biggest, most established, and accredited by many regulatory bodies as a travel agency specializing in discount international airline tickets, offering consolidator fares for flights originating in Sri Lanka to many international locations in Asia, Africa, Central and South America, Europe, the Middle East, the South Pacific, the USA, Canada, and the Indian Subcontinent. We provide different discounted domestic and international airfares for all countries worldwide. We distribute international tickets for some of the top airlines in the world.
